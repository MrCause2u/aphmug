About Our Ministry

Founded in faith, growing in love

Our Foundation

Founded on May 25th, 2015, by Pastor Kabagambe Aloyzious and his family, God's Embassy Church has been a beacon of hope in Kyenjojo district, Uganda. What started as a vision has grown into a thriving community of faith.

Our Community

We are a home to 180+ believers who come together to worship Christ, and over 250 children who study the Bible twice a month. Our ministry continues to grow as more souls are led to Christ.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and bringing love to the needy. Through healing, hope, and transformation, we serve the lame, barren, and those in need of spiritual guidance.

Our Ministry Focus

Spreading the love of Christ in action

Gospel Preaching

Spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ as commanded in Mark 16:15, reaching communities where Christ is less known.

Healing Ministry

Through prayer and faith, we minister healing to the sick, hope to the hopeless, and transformation to all who seek.

Baptisms

Over the years, many people have been baptized and led to Christ by Pastor Aloysius, marking their new life in Jesus.

Community Outreach

We actively reach out to the needy in our community, providing spiritual guidance and practical support.

Prophetic Ministry

Through the gift of prophecy, we provide spiritual insight and guidance to believers seeking God's direction.

Church Fellowship

Creating a warm, welcoming environment where believers can grow in faith and fellowship together in Christ.

Children's Bible Study

Nurturing young hearts for Christ

In 2024, we established the Children's Bible Study initiative to enhance better understanding of God's word among children. This program helps children follow the ways of the Lord and prepares them to go into the world to spread the gospel.
