Life-Changing Ministry

Aloysius Prophetic and Healing Ministries (APHM) is dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and the love of Christ to the needy. Join our faith-based, Born Again Christian community for healing, hope, and transformation.

About Us

Founded by Pastor Kabagambe Aloyzious and his family on May 25, 2015, APHM (God's Embassy Church) is located in GR2R+87 Lwebali, Kyenjojo district, Uganda. We are home to 180+ believers and over 250 children studying the Bible twice a month. Our mission is to lead people to Christ, provide healing, and support the needy.

Children's Bible Study
